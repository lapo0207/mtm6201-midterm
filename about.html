Who Are We?

Our company is built on family values and hard work. We are the fifth generation of proud owners. Our commitment to quality and flavour makes us stand out from all the others.


Our ice cream comes from a recipe passed down from generation to generation. We use nothing but the finnest, purest, natural ingredients. Today we add our own little twist to make every flavour unique. We invite you to come try us out for yourselves and see the Ice Cream Palace difference.
